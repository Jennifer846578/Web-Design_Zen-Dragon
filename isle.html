1

Isle of Berk

The Isle of Berk, also known as Berk and Old Berk, is a relatively small island that serves as the primary setting for the DreamWorks Dragons franchise. This island was previously home to the many Vikings of the Hooligan Tribe and many dragons as well.

2

Death's Head Headland

Death's Head Headland was the site of the Battle at Death's Head Headland. Before the battle, Hiccup came up with a "fiendishly clever plan" to defeat both the Green Death and the Purple Death. The Hooligans used their hunting dragons to lure the Green Death to Death's Head Headland, and the Meatheads used theirs to do the same with the Purple Death. The plan worked at first, and the Green Death killed the Purple Death, but then the Green Death emerged from the sea and started hunting for Hiccup, or "little supper."

3

Black Hearth Bay

Located on the southern shores of Berk, this large bay is surrounded by giant cliffs and isles. It's the perfect habitat for the Thunderdrum who's comfortable both in and out of the water.

4

Isle of Doom

The Isle of Doom is craggy and inhospitable nightmare lands to anyone who is not already a Deathgripper. The only purpose these islands had for Vikings was to send criminals there as a punishment. For some Dragons, however, the isle is a paradise.

5

Meat Head Island
(South Island)

The Meathead Islands is a small archipelago of islands located in the Barbaric Archipelago. The Meathead Islands were the home of the Meathead Tribe prior to the Second Dragon War. It can be presumed that the Meatheads returned though at after it's end.

6

Meat Head Island
(North Island)

Meathead Island is a fictional locale known for its quirky charm and laid-back atmosphere. Situated off the coast, it boasts sandy beaches, swaying palm trees, and crystal-clear waters perfect for surfing and swimming. The island is home to a colorful cast of characters, including surfers, beach bums, and free spirits who embrace the island's motto: "Life's too short to stress." Visitors can enjoy fresh seafood shacks, beachside bonfires, and vibrant sunsets that paint the sky in hues of orange and pink. With its relaxed vibe and welcoming community, Meathead Island is the ultimate destination for those seeking a break from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.
